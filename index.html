About

I'm a creative technologist with a passion for design and a keen eye for detail. My journey began as a photographer and graphic designer, where I developed a strong foundation in visual storytelling and a love for crafting visually striking designs.

With a Master's degree in Data Science and AI for the Creative Industries (Distinction), I've expanded my skillset to include advanced AI techniques. I'm particularly interested in exploring the intersection of art and technology, using AI as a tool to enhance creativity and efficiency.

My thesis project, a collaboration with a renowned costume designer, focused on using AI to transform hand-drawn sketches into photorealistic renderings. This project not only showcased the potential of AI in fashion design but also highlighted the importance of ethical AI practices and sustainable fashion.

I'm excited to apply my skills and knowledge to a variety of design roles, from creating captivating book covers to developing innovative AI-powered design tools. I'm eager to work with like-minded individuals who share my passion for pushing the boundaries of creativity and technology.

From Sketch to Reality

AI-Enhanced Co-Design for Genderless Fashion

This project explores the integration of artificial intelligence into the fashion design process through a co-design framework. In collaboration with an independent garment designer, the project focuses on creating genderless fashion visualisations using bespoke datasets composed of the designer's hand-drawn sketches and my own original photography.

The gallery below features:

  • Original sketches and photography
  • Generative outputs (sketches + photoreal renders)
  • Final post-processed results

AI tools used: Stable Diffusion, ControlNet, DreamBooth.
